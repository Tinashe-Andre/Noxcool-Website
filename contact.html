Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services

How long does installation take?

Installation time varies by product type. Air conditioning units typically take 2-4 hours, while appliances like fridges and washing machines usually take 1-2 hours. We'll provide a specific timeline during your consultation.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes! We offer flexible financing options through our partner banks. You can spread payments over 12, 24, or 36 months with competitive interest rates. Contact us for more details.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide comprehensive warranties on all products and installations. Standard warranty is 2 years on appliances and 5 years on air conditioning systems, with extended warranty options available.

Do you service all brands?

We service most major brands including Samsung, LG, Bosch, Whirlpool, and more. Our certified technicians are trained on multiple brands and can handle most repair and maintenance needs.
